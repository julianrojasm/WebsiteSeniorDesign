About The Project

Motivation

  • We Live In Florida, Which Means We Are Surrounded By Water.
  • CREOL Team Member.
  • There Are No Small Buoys For Recreational Such As Boating, Fishing, Surfing.
  • Small And Portable Commercial Buoy For Surveing and Researching.
    •

Goals

  • Small Scale Smart Buoy
  • Cost Efficient
  • Solar Powered
  • Ease of Use With Mobile Application for Monitoring Data
  • Functionality includes: Water and Air Temperature, Wind Speed, Salinity Detection, Realtime 3D Movement View
